Our Journey

The Beginning

KeyVista was founded in 2019 by Anwar & Mizan, two visionaries with over a decade of experience in real estate and technology. What began as a side project to simplify property transactions quickly grew into a mission to transform the entire industry.

Our first platform launched in April 2020, connecting home seekers with verified properties during a time when physical viewings became challenging.

Our Vision

KeyVista was built on a simple principle: real estate should be transparent, accessible, and stress-free. We combine cutting-edge technology with deep market knowledge to create the most user-friendly property platform.

"Our mission is to make finding your dream home as easy as browsing your favorite online store - with all the trust and none of the hassle."

First Success Story

We received our first property listing request the day our landing page went live. By July 2020, we had helped our first client find their perfect home without a single physical visit - proving that technology could make real estate better.

This early validation fueled our passion to innovate further in the property market.

The Breakthrough

When we launched publicly in August 2020, we quickly became one of the fastest-growing real estate platforms, serving over 250 clients in our first week. Our unique approach combining virtual tours, AI recommendations, and honest pricing resonated with home seekers nationwide.

Today, we're proud to have helped thousands of families find their perfect homes while maintaining our startup spirit of innovation.

Why "KeyVista"?

Our name combines "Key" - representing both property keys and solutions - with "Vista" - symbolizing the beautiful views and new perspectives we help create. Together, they embody our promise: unlocking your perfect living space with a vision for your future.

Just like the ancient Greek architectural orders that inspired us, we believe in building something that stands the test of time.
